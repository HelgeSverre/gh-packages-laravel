# Laravel bKash Payment Gateway

[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)](LICENSE)

A simple and configurable **Laravel package** to integrate **bKash payments**. Supports payment creation, execution, refunds, webhooks, and middleware-based verification.

---

## 📦 Installation

```bash
composer require abdursoft/lara-bkash

## publish vendor

```bash
php artisan vendor:publish --tag=bkash-config
